## About

TUI nav is a hugo theme I created specifically for this very website. Recently the TUI has seen a bit of a resurgence in popularity. Its minimalist style and quick keyboard based navigation make it a pleasure to use. I wanted to bring that same charm and feel to my personal website. Check it out [here!](https://github.com/pachuc/tui-nav)
